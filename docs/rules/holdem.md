# Texas Hold’em (Baseline Rules)

## Objective
Win the pot by having the best 5-card hand at showdown or by making all opponents fold.

## Setup
- 2–10 players.
- Dealer button rotates each hand.
- Forced blinds: Small Blind (SB) and Big Blind (BB).

## Hand Flow
1. **Post blinds**
2. **Preflop**: deal 2 hole cards, betting round.
3. **Flop**: deal 3 community cards, betting round.
4. **Turn**: deal 1 community card, betting round.
5. **River**: deal 1 community card, betting round.
6. **Showdown**: best 5-card hand wins (split pot if tie).

## Actions
Check, Bet, Call, Raise, Fold, All-in.

## Hand Rankings (High → Low)
1. Straight Flush (Royal Flush is top)
2. Four of a Kind
3. Full House
4. Flush
5. Straight
6. Three of a Kind
7. Two Pair
8. One Pair
9. High Card

## Betting Structure (V1 Choice)
**No-Limit Hold’em**:
- Any raise size up to all-in.
- Min raise must be at least the size of the previous bet/raise.

## Side Pots
If a player is all-in and others continue betting, create side pots. All-in player can only win the main pot.

## Variants to Decide
- Table size
- Blind sizes and structure
- Limit vs No-limit vs Pot-limit
