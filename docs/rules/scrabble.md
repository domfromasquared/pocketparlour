# Scrabble (Baseline Rules)

## Objective
Score the most points by forming valid words on a 15×15 board.

## Setup
- Each player draws 7 tiles.
- First move must cover center star.
- Tiles placed in a single row or column.

## Placement Rules
- Words must be horizontal or vertical.
- All new words formed (main + cross) must be valid.
- Moves must connect to existing tiles after the first move.

## Scoring
1. Sum letter values.
2. Apply letter multipliers for newly placed tiles.
3. Apply word multipliers for newly placed tiles.
4. Score all cross words formed.
5. **Bingo**: +50 for using all 7 tiles.

## Tile Values (English)
- 1 point: E A I O N R T L S U
- 2 points: D G
- 3 points: B C M P
- 4 points: F H V W Y
- 5 points: K
- 8 points: J X
- 10 points: Q Z
- 0 points: blanks (wild)

## Endgame
When tiles are exhausted and a player goes out:
- That player gains the sum of opponents’ remaining tiles.
- Opponents subtract remaining tile points.

## Variants to Decide
- Dictionary (TWL/NWL vs CSW).
- Challenge rules.
- Exchange allowed only if 7+ tiles in bag (standard).
